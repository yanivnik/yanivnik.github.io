I'm a CS PhD student at the [Technion](https://www.technion.ac.il/en/home-2/), where I'm advised by Prof. [Yonatan Belinkov](https://belinkov.com/). My research interests revolve around Deep Learning and interpretability. I aim to study the properties and internals of neural networks, and utilize these understandings to build better models.

I completed my M.Sc under the supervision of [Prof. Michal Irani](https://www.weizmann.ac.il/math/irani), and prior to that I've worked as a software engineer in industry and received my B.Sc in Compuer science.
